Completionist Checklist, Crystal Inheritance v1.0.0

* Reach B2F of Union Cave 
* Return the wayward Nomad in the Vortex Caverns 
* Solve all the Ruins of Alph puzzles
* Solve the Violet Catacombs puzzle
* Help the old man in Gauldenrod
* Awaken the ancestor Xatu, Magcargo, Meganium, and Feraligatr
* Solve all five of the Kimono Cabin puzzles
* Collect all 14 badges
* Reach the bottom of the Enigma Glacier
* Open the Slowpoke Well B2F chamber
* Save the Route KaJo schoolgirl
* Complete the Master Ball trade quest
* Read your Rival's letter to his father 
* Beat the generals on hardmode after you reach the credits once
* Collect all TM and HM 
* Complete the Pokedex
* Find all four hidden grottoes
* Defeat Cynthia (Mahogany Gate), Artist Mario (Goldenrod Museum), dwg (Debug Room)
